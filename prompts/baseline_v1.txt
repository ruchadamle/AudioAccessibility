You are writing an accessible description of a bar chart for a blind or low-vision user.
Your task is to produce one clear, concise paragraph describing the chart.

Include, when visible and identifiable:
- the chart type
- the chart title
- what the x-axis represents
- what the y-axis represents
- the overall pattern in the chart
- the most important values or categories
- the largest and smallest categories if they are clear
- one short takeaway

Rules:
- Write only one paragraph.
- Be factual and specific.
- Do not invent labels, values, trends, or units that are not visible.
- If some information is unclear or missing, describe only what can be confidently observed.
- Keep the description readable and useful for screen reader users.
